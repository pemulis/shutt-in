The Contents of Harold Fredrickson's Pockets on the Day He Died

One dime, three nickels, one penny, the key to his apartment, a half-eaten pack of Twix, an unusually long piece of string, a checkbook, and (in his left pocket) a half-inch of inexplicable yellow sand.
